Achilles

Hack and slash your way through 15 stages of greek warriors. Violent game containing decapitations, impalements, knee breaks, leg chops etc. Enjoy! NOTE: Just to clarify, the enemies' armour doesnt increase each level, and they dont get quicker or better weapons.
The only thing that increases is the difficulty of the CPU.

Gameplay buttons

‚å®

  • W or Up arrow key to Jump.
  • A or Left arrow key to Walk left
  • D or Right arrow key to Walk Right
  • S or Down arrow key to Block/Pick up spear (when near a spear)
  • T or Number 4 to swipe with Xiphos sword.
  • K or Number 5 to kick

Tips

Block arrows with your shield by pressing s or down.
Disable enemies temporarily by kicking them.
Spears are most effective when thrown.
Revisit earlier levels to get more lives for later on.
